Data Privacy & Security: Built-In Solution

One of the biggest pain points that drives customers to Atlas: data privacy. Our platform makes privacy management simple through policy-driven controls and governance.

The Problem

Teams send sensitive customer data to external APIs (OpenAI, Cohere) without controls. No way to enforce "customer data can only go to internal APIs." Compliance risk.

Our Solution

Policy engine classifies data sensitivity. Enforces routing: "PII goes to internal APIs only, public data ok with external." Auto-blocks violations at deployment.

Customer Benefit

Privacy is no longer a security review bottleneck. It's built into the platform. Teams move faster. Compliance officers sleep better.

Privacy Controls in Atlas

🔒

Data Classification

Mark data as Public, Internal, Sensitive, PII. Policies route by classification.

🔒

API Allowlists

"Customer data can ONLY go to: internal embedding API, not OpenAI."

🔒

Automatic Enforcement

Model tries to violate policy → deployment blocked → team gets remediation suggestion.

🔒

Audit Trail

Complete log: who accessed what data, when, where it went. SOC 2 ready.
