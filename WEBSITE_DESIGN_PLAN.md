# Atlas AI Hub - Website Design Plan & Prompts

**Status:** Design Planning Phase  
**Created:** May 6, 2026  
**Design Aesthetic:** Enterprise Bold  
**Target Audiences:** AI Managers + CFOs + End Users (equal emphasis)  
**Brand Colors:** Teal (#065A82) + Navy (#1E2761) + Coral (#F96167)

---

## 🎨 Design System

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Navy #1E2761 (trust, authority, enterprise)
- **Secondary:** Teal #065A82 (innovation, tech, governance)
- **Accent:** Coral #F96167 (energy, action, CTAs)
- **Neutral Light:** #F5F7FA (backgrounds, cards)
- **Neutral Dark:** #36454F (text, contrast)
- **White:** #FFFFFF (content areas)

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Calibri or Arial Bold, 36-48pt (page titles), 24-28pt (section headers)
- **Body:** Calibri, 14-16pt (readability)
- **UI Copy:** 12-14pt (consistent throughout)
- **Accent/Taglines:** Italic, 16-18pt (key messaging)

### Layout Principles
- **Dominance:** 60% primary color (navy/teal), 20% neutral, 20% accent (coral)
- **Grid:** 12-column responsive grid (desktop), 4-column (mobile)
- **Spacing:** 0.5" margins minimum, 0.3-0.5" between sections
- **Visual Motifs:**
  - Icons in colored circles (navy, teal, coral)
  - Statement blocks with gradients (navy to teal)
  - Bordered content cards with teal accent lines
  - Data visualization callouts for metrics

### Component Style Guide
- **Buttons:** Rounded corners (8px), full-width CTAs on mobile
  - Primary CTA: Coral background, white text, 16pt
  - Secondary CTA: Teal outline, navy text
- **Cards:** White background, subtle shadow, teal top border (3px)
- **Forms:** Navy labels, teal input focus states, coral submit button
- **Hero Sections:** Navy or teal gradient background with white text overlay
- **Stats/Metrics:** Large numbers (40-48pt), small labels (12pt), colored backgrounds

## 🎯 Website-Wide Design Principles

### Navigation & Headers
- **Top Navigation Bar:**
  - Logo (left): "Atlas AI Hub" wordmark in navy
  - Menu items (right, navy text): Home | Product | Pricing | About | Resources
  - CTA button (right, coral): "Get Started" or "Login"
  - Background: White with subtle bottom border (light gray)
  - Sticky on scroll (desktop), hamburger menu on mobile
  
### Buttons & CTAs
- **Primary CTA (Coral):** "Start Free Trial", "Get Early Access", "Schedule Demo"
- **Secondary CTA (Teal):** "Learn More", "Explore Features", "Contact Sales"
- **Tertiary (Navy Outline):** "View Docs", "View Pricing"
- All buttons: Rounded corners (8px), 16-18pt font, hover state with color shift

### Forms
- Input fields: Navy border (2px), light gray background, teal focus border
- Labels: Navy text, 12pt, bold
- Placeholder text: Light gray, 14pt
- Submit button: Coral, full-width on mobile, 16pt bold
- Validation: Red error text below field, red border on invalid state

### Footer
- Dark background (navy #1E2761)
- White text
- 4-5 columns: Product Links | Company | Legal | Social | Newsletter signup
- Newsletter signup: Input + "Subscribe" button (coral)
- Copyright & links at bottom

### Mobile Responsiveness
- All sections stack vertically on < 768px
- 1-column for cards (2-3 columns on desktop)
- Hero images hidden on mobile (text only)
- Forms: Full width with 12-16px padding
